Thinking of Buying Refurbished Appliances? What You Need To Know

Top Tips from Melbourne's best Electrician


When it comes to purchasing appliances, many consumers are hesitant to consider buying refurbished products. However, purchasing refurbished appliances can be a smart financial decision, as long as you take the time to thoroughly research and inspect the product before making your purchase. Here are a few tips to help you make an informed decision when buying refurbished appliances.

Do Your Research

It is highly recommended you do not rush to make a purchase! One of the most important steps in buying refurbished appliances is to research the product and the seller before making your purchase. Look for reviews and ratings of the product, as well as the seller's reputation and warranty policies. Also, make sure to research the product's original retail price, so you can compare it to the refurbished price and ensure that you are getting a good deal. Electrical appliances may have been repaired on the cheap by someone who was not a trained technician. Check for Damage

This might seem really obvious but before making your purchase, inspect the refurbished appliance for any signs of damage or wear. This includes checking for dents, scratches, or other cosmetic damage, as well as testing the appliance to ensure that it is in proper working order. If the seller is unwilling to let you inspect the product or does not provide detailed information about its condition, it may be best to look for another seller. Make sure you check the cords and any signs of damage or wear or repair.

Look for a Warranty

When purchasing a refurbished appliance, it is important to look for a warranty that will protect you in case the product does not work as expected. Many reputable sellers will offer a warranty on their refurbished products, so make sure to look for this when making your purchase. You may find the original warranty has run out so check with the supplier whether a warranty can be extended on the device or equipment prior to purchase. For more detail on consumer laws for second-hand electronic appliances, you can find this on the Energy Safe Victoria website.

Consider the Brand

When buying refurbished appliances, it is important to consider the brand of the product. Some brands are known for producing high-quality, durable appliances, while others may be prone to breaking down or needing repairs. Researching different brands and their reputations can help you make an informed decision when purchasing a refurbished appliance. People who are unhappy generally vent their frustration online in the form of forums and reviews. It is wise to check for these to get a feel of what has gone right or wrong with the electrical equipment for others.

Compare Prices

When buying refurbished appliances, it is important to compare prices from different sellers before making your purchase. This will help you ensure that you are getting the best deal possible, and can also help you find the lowest price for the product you are looking for. But remember that the lowest price may not get you the best electrically sound equipment. It is worth keeping the price in mind but this may not end up being the deciding factor.

Trust your gut

Lastly, trust your gut instinct when buying refurbished appliances. If something seems off or if the seller seems shady, it may be best to look for a different option. It is important to remember that purchasing refurbished appliances can be a great financial decision, as long as you take the time to thoroughly research and inspect the product before making your purchase.

Turn it on before buying

If you are in a situation where you can ask - request the seller turn the device or equipment on so that you can see, hear and feel if the equipment is in good working order. Try different settings and turn it up and down to understand if it is able to function as it should safely.

Ensure it is tested

Ensure the equipment is put into the schedule for test and tag when the technicians come to your business. That way you will be able to ensure you have a record of its history since you purchased it and keep your team safe. Make an informed choice

By following these tips, you can make an informed decision when purchasing refurbished appliances and feel confident that you are getting a high-quality product at a fair price. Remember to research the product, the seller, and the warranty before making your purchase, and to carefully inspect the product for any signs of damage or wear. Additionally, consider the brand of the product, compare prices from different sellers, and trust your gut instinct when making your final decision.
